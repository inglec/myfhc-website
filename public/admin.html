Adding a YouTube Video

Click 'Share' and copy the link of the form:

https://youtu.be/unique-id

Adding a Streamable Video

Copy the URL from your browser of the form:

https://www.streamable.com/unique-id

Adding a Twitch Video

Copy the URL from your browser of the form:

https://www.twitch.tv/videos/unique-id

Adding a Facebook Video

Right click on the video while it's playing, and select 'Show video URL'. Copy this link.


Adding a Facebook Poll

Click on the timestamp of the post. When you are re-directed to the poll, copy the URL from your browser.


Editing the Database

1) If there is an error in a video or post, you can go to the Firebase console.

2) Select the "MYFH Crew" project, and then the "Database" tab on the left hand side of the page.

3) Make sure that the database selected is the "Cloud Firestore" database.

4) Go to the relevant table, and find the entry you want to edit.

You can find out the unique ID of a video from its URL.

For YouTube videos, their entry will be called:

yt=unique-id

For Streamable videos:

st=unique-id

For Facebook videos:

fb=unique-id

For Facebook polls:

timestamp-of-poll

For posts:

timestamp-of-post

To add a tag, click the "+ Add Field" symbol beside the tags entry.

Name the tag, and set the "Type" to "null".
